# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designers

### Role Summary
UX Designers create intuitive, accessible user experiences by designing workflows, prototypes, and interfaces that align with project goals and user needs. They bridge product strategy with user-centered design practices.

### Responsibilities
- Design user flows, wireframes, and prototypes to validate concepts
- Conduct user research and incorporate feedback into designs
- Collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to ensure feasibility and usability
- Participate in design reviews and iterate based on feedback
- Define accessibility and design standards for the project
- Document design decisions and maintain design systems

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user interfaces
- Reduce friction in user workflows
- Ensure designs align with product vision and user needs
- Build shared understanding across teams through design artifacts

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions with Product Managers and Developers
- User research findings and usability test results
- Design specifications and component documentation
- Collaboration in planning meetings to flag design implications early

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Works with Product Managers** to understand user needs and validate design solutions
- **Works with Developers** to ensure designs are technically feasible and well-documented
- **Works with Project Managers** to align design timelines with project schedules
- **Collaborates with Data Analysts** to understand user behavior and metrics

---

## DevOps Engineers

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment systems that enable reliable, frequent delivery of software. They ensure production systems are stable, observable, and secure.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines for automated testing and deployment
- Configure and manage infrastructure, monitoring, and alerting systems
- Ensure security best practices in deployment and infrastructure
- Automate testing and deployment processes to reduce manual effort
- Collaborate with Developers and QA to troubleshoot and resolve deployment issues
- Participate in incident response and post-mortems
- Document runbooks and operational procedures

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and frequent deployments
- Minimize deployment-related failures and incidents
- Reduce manual operational overhead
- Maintain observability and quick incident detection

### Typical Communication
- Deployment schedules and release coordination with Project Managers
- Pipeline and infrastructure improvements with Developers and QA
- Incident notifications and post-incident reviews
- Documentation of deployment procedures and operational runbooks

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Works with Developers** to integrate code changes into CI/CD pipelines and resolve build failures
- **Works with QA/Testing** to automate test execution and ensure quality gates in deployment
- **Works with Project Managers** on release scheduling, deployment windows, and rollback procedures
- **Works with Security Lead** to implement security scanning and compliance in pipelines

---

## Data Analysts

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define, track, and interpret metrics that measure project success and user behavior. They provide data-driven insights to inform product decisions and demonstrate business impact.

### Responsibilities
- Define key success metrics aligned with project goals
- Set up tracking, dashboards, and reporting infrastructure
- Analyze data to measure feature impact and user behavior
- Identify trends and patterns to inform product improvements
- Partner with Product Managers to validate hypotheses and guide prioritization
- Create regular reports and communicate findings to stakeholders
- Help establish baselines and success criteria

### Goals
- Demonstrate measurable project impact
- Inform product decisions with data
- Enable evidence-based prioritization and iteration
- Build transparency into project outcomes

### Typical Communication
- Metrics and dashboard reviews with Product Managers
- Analysis reports and findings with Project Managers and stakeholders
- Data-driven insights to support sprint planning and retrospectives
- Collaboration during project initiation to establish success metrics

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Works with Product Managers** to define success metrics and measure feature impact
- **Works with Project Managers** to report on key performance indicators and project health
- **Works with Developers** to understand technical implementation of tracking and data collection
- **Works with UX Designers** to analyze user behavior and validate design decisions

---

## Customer Success / Support Representatives

### Role Summary
Customer Success Representatives bridge the gap between the product team and customers. They gather direct customer feedback, communicate product updates, and ensure customer needs are understood throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and communicate customer feedback to product and development teams
- Identify customer pain points and feature requests
- Provide early access or beta testing opportunities to customers
- Communicate known issues, workarounds, and fixes post-launch
- Serve as the primary liaison for customer communication during incidents
- Document customer use cases and success stories
- Participate in sprint planning to advocate for customer needs

### Goals
- Ensure customer voice is heard in product decisions
- Reduce time-to-resolution for customer issues
- Build strong customer relationships and retention
- Identify opportunities for customer value and impact

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback and insights during planning and retrospectives
- Early access and beta communication with select customers
- Post-launch release notes and known issues documentation
- Incident communication and status updates to affected customers

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Works with Product Managers** to advocate for customer needs and validate feature requests
- **Works with Developers** to understand technical capabilities and limitations
- **Works with Project Managers** to escalate critical customer issues and urgent requests
- **Works with UX Designers** to provide user feedback on usability and design

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads ensure that security is built into projects from the start. They conduct threat assessments, review features for security implications, and coordinate incident response to protect customer data and systems.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct threat modeling and risk assessments for new features
- Review architecture and code for security vulnerabilities
- Ensure compliance with security policies and standards
- Participate in design and architecture reviews
- Coordinate security testing and vulnerability scanning
- Lead incident response for security issues
- Provide security guidance and training to the team
- Maintain security documentation and runbooks

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities from reaching production
- Reduce security incident impact and response time
- Build security awareness across the team
- Maintain customer trust through secure practices

### Typical Communication
- Threat modeling and security review findings with Developers and Architects
- Security incident notifications and coordinated response
- Policy and compliance updates with Project Managers and stakeholders
- Security best practices and training with the broader team

### Interaction with Existing Roles
- **Works with Developers** to review code and architecture for security issues
- **Works with DevOps Engineers** to implement security scanning and secure infrastructure practices
- **Works with Project Managers** to coordinate security incidents and compliance requirements
- **Works with Product Managers** to understand security implications of feature requirements

- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- When planning projects, identify which personas should be involved based on scope and complexity.
- Use persona interactions to understand cross-functional dependencies and communication needs.
